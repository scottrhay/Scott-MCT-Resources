For the suggested prompts, use the following: 

Interested Prospect:
Create a follow-up email for a prospect who is interested but needs time to decide. Include next steps and maintain momentum.

Ready to Buy:
Draft an enthusiastic follow-up for a client ready to move forward. Include contract next steps and onboarding timeline.

Missed Meeting:
Write a friendly email to reschedule with someone who missed our meeting. Keep tone understanding and professional.

Proposal Check-in:
Create a follow-up email checking on a proposal sent last week. Be helpful not pushy.

Thank You Email:
Write a thank you email after first meeting with a potential client. Reference what we discussed and suggest next steps.

Not Now Response:
Draft a professional response for a prospect who says the timing isn't right. Keep door open for future.
